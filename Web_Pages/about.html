A Legacy Born in 1950

Formula One World Championship began in 1950 at Silverstone Circuit in the United Kingdom. What started as a European-centric championship has evolved into a global phenomenon, captivating millions of fans across every continent.

From the legendary drivers like Juan Manuel Fangio and Ayrton Senna to modern champions like Lewis Hamilton and Max Verstappen, F1 has consistently pushed the boundaries of speed, technology, and human performance.
