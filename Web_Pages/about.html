The 'Nimble' Chassis

Cars are now significantly smaller to promote better wheel-to-wheel racing. The wheelbase has been shortened from 3600mm to 3400mm, the width reduced from 2000mm to 1900mm, and the minimum weight slashed by 30kg. This makes the cars much more agile through tight street circuits.

Redefined Power Unit

The complex MGU-H has been entirely banned. Instead, the 1.6-liter turbocharged V6 engine is now paired with a massively upscaled 350kW MGU-K. This means electrical energy now provides roughly 50% of the car's 1000+ total horsepower, making battery harvesting strategies critical.

Active Aerodynamics

For the first time ever, both the front and rear wings feature active, moving flaps. Drivers switch between Z-Mode (which opens the flaps for high downforce in corners) and X-Mode (which flattens the wings for low drag and extreme top speed on straights).

100% Sustainable Fuel
