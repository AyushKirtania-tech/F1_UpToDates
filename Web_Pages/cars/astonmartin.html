The AMR25 is powered by Mercedes-AMG's latest turbocharged 1.6-liter V6 internal combustion engine, paired with sophisticated hybrid technology. The dual Motor Generator Units (MGU-K and MGU-H) work seamlessly to recover energy during braking and from exhaust gases, contributing around 160 HP of electrical boost. This integrated power unit delivers approximately 1,000 horsepower combined, revving to 15,000 RPM with exceptional reliability proven over multiple seasons of competition.

Careful attention to airflow management through the sidepods and around the rear end ensures stable performance across varying speeds and circuit configurations.
