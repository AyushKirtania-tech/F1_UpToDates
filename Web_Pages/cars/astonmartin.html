Overview

The Aston Martin AMR26 marks the dawn of a massive new era for the Silverstone-based team. Transitioning from a Mercedes customer to the exclusive factory partner of Honda Racing Corporation, and designed under the guidance of legendary aerodynamicist Adrian Newey, the AMR26 represents Lawrence Stroll's ultimate vision. With a brand-new wind tunnel online and the 2026 active aerodynamics regulations in effect, this is Aston Martin's definitive bid for World Championship glory.
