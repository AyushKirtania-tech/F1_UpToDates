Overview

The Mercedes W16 is Mercedes-AMG Petronas' 2025 challenger. Introduced as the next step after the W15, the W16 focuses on resolving low-speed turn-in and tyre temperature consistency while improving aerodynamic efficiency and mechanical grip. The car marks a new era for the team with a refreshed line-up and updated technical package.
