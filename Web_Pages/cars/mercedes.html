Overview

The Mercedes-AMG F1 W16 E Performance is Mercedes-AMG Petronas' 2025 challenger. The car builds on lessons from earlier Mercedes designs with a focus on aerodynamic efficiency, structural stiffness, and refined thermal management for the hybrid power unit. The W16 is intended to balance high-speed stability with strong mid-corner performance while providing a robust platform for in-season development.
